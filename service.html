Temaco Services

The main business of TEMACO Builders is concrete manufacturing. In order to facilitate and care most for its customers, TEMACO offers different services related to products it supplies such as ground preparation, ground leveling, supply of laterite, compacting, sand supply, supply of aggregate, tiles & paving stones fixation and transportation.

Transportation

Temaco Builders offers delivery services to its customers after purchasing its products. Additionally, Temaco Builders also has the ability of supplying laterite, aggregate, and sand in case a customer needs them. Last but not least, all the costs related to transportation are negotiated on their own with the company.

Ground Preparation and Installation

Temaco Builders offers employees to do the ground preparation before installing the bought products. In addition, the employees also have the ability of installing and fixing all the Temaco products including paving stones and tiles. Last but not least, the costs for both ground preparation and installation are negotiated on their own with the company.
